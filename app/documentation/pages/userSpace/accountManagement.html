Account Management

You can manage all your account information through the User Space. Different features are available.


  • Profile : you can modify your personal information, including your name, surname, telephone number and billing information.

  • Change Password : you can change your password through this view.

  • Billing : this view lists all your bills and offers you the possibility to change your payment account as well as settling your unpaid payments.

  • Tickets : This view lists all the tickets you have submitted to the support as well as different information allowing you to follow up quickly and easily.
