Wakanda Support

Wakanda Support offers two support plans:

  • Wakanda support
  • Cloud support

    • These support plans are available to all developers, regardless of the type of license; community, business, or otherwise. The two plans of support have been designed to give you the amount and availability of support you require for your projects. The level of support depends on the type of your subscription in order to provide the level of support that meets your needs.

    Accessing Wakanda Support

    In order to access Wakanda support you need to use wakanda console. Simply sign in at Wakanda console and enter your email address and password.

    In Wakanda console click on support in the navigation bar and you will be redirected to wakanda Help Center console.

    • Submit a request: Create a new request. The request can be for the Wakanda support or the Cloud one.
    • Frequently asked questions: Browse through these FAQs to find answers to commonly raised questions.
    • Profil: View the history of your activities and sign out from the Wakanda Help center console.

    Submit a request

    To submit a request, click on submit request and choose the type of support you need; Wakanda support or Cloud support

    Exemple: Cloud support

    Here is an example of submitting a Cloud support request. The lists that follow the form example explain some of your options.

    • Subject: The subject of your request. Treat this like the subject of an email message and sum up your issue as briefly as possible.
    • Description: This is the most important information that you provide to Support. For most service and category combinations, a prompt suggests information that is most helpful for the fastest resolution.
    • Attachments: Screen shots and other attachments files are often helpful.
