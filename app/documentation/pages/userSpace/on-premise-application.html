On-premise APP

To create a new on-premise app, click on New Subscription link in the left side menu:

Create premium on-premise application

COULD is selected by default in the tab panel, click on ON-PREM link to select on premise:

For this example, we choose silver plan. Choose your plan and click on subscribe to continue to the next step:

To make it easy for you to identify each subscirpiton in your subscriptions list, we give you the possiblity to name your subscriptions. Hostnames are used to generate your unique license, valid hostnames must contain one main domain name and as many of sub-domains as you want (for example: test.com, a.test.com, b.test.com):

Click on the next button to continue to your subscription summary and license agreement validation view. Read and agree to our Terms of Service, then click on Pay With PayPal button to complete your subscription:

Now, you should be redirected to your subscriptions list with the new created one under ON-PREMISE APPLICATIONS section:

Create dev on-premise application

Our dev plan allows you to develop your application and try Wakanda platform in your local machine with the possibility to upgrade to one of our premium at anytime. To select dev plan, click on subscribe button:

Rather than app name and hostnames used in the previous section, for dev app, you only need to provide your application name. Go ahead and insert the name of your choice and click next:

You should now be on your subscription summary and license agreement validation view, just complete the process like we have done in the previous section.
